Common questions

FAQ

Use these answers to reduce support requests and increase confidence before checkout.

How do customers buy from the site?

Replace each placeholder product button with a Stripe payment link, Shopify Buy Button, Ecwid product link, or embedded checkout component.

Can fulfillment be automated?

Yes. Connect a print-on-demand provider so they receive the order details, produce the item, and ship it to the customer for you.

Do I need to hold inventory?

No. A print-on-demand setup lets you avoid buying inventory upfront, though margins will be lower than bulk ordering yourself.

Can I add more products later?

Yes. Add additional product cards, duplicate sections, or swap the current grid for an embedded storefront once your catalog grows.
